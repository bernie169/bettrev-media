Setup Guide — how to wire everything up

1

Replace your BeMob destination URL

Go to BeMob → Edit Campaign → Destination URL. Replace it with the Visit Tracker URL shown above for your brand. This single URL records the visit AND forwards to Easybet with the click ID attached. The lp= value should be changed per campaign to identify your landing page variant (e.g. lp=spinwheel or lp=scratchcard).

2

Send postback URLs to Easybet

Copy the Registration, FTD and Deposit postback URLs from the panel above and send them to your Easybet affiliate contact. They plug these into their system. When a player converts, Easybet fires back to your tracker automatically.

3

Mondiad dual-fire is automatic

When an FTD postback is received, this tracker automatically fires back to Mondiad at postback.pbmnd.com with your uid and the click ID. No BeMob involvement needed for this step — it's built in.

4

Test a conversion

Click through your BeMob campaign URL manually (use real URL, not test URL). Copy the click ID from BeMob → Live Visits. Go to Mondiad → Conversion Tracking → Test, paste that click ID. You should see a visit appear here immediately, and a test FTD appear once Mondiad fires back.

5

Adding a new brand

Click + Add brand in the sidebar. Fill in name, slug (e.g. hollywoodbets), currency (ZAR, USD, EUR), pick a colour. Postback URLs are generated instantly. Send them to the new brand's affiliate team.
