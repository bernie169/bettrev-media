Setup Guide
1

Propeller Ads Campaign URL

Destination URL: https://bettrev-media.vercel.app/api/visit?brand=easybet&cid={SUBID}&s1={ZONEID}&campaign_id={CAMPAIGN_ID}&network=propeller&dest=https://ebpartners.click/o/lWk0jB?lpage=_HWky_

2

Postbacks are already live with Easybet

Easybet fires registration, FTD and deposit postbacks automatically. The tracker detects the network and fires to Propeller Ads or Mondiad accordingly.
