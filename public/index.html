Setup Guide — how to wire everything up

1

Propeller Ads Campaign URL

Use this as your Propeller Ads destination URL:
https://bettrev-media.vercel.app/api/visit?brand=easybet&cid={SUBID}&s1={ZONEID}&campaign_id={CAMPAIGN_ID}&network=propeller&dest=https://ebpartners.click/o/lWk0jB?lpage=_HWky_

2

Send postback URLs to Easybet

Copy the Registration, FTD and Deposit postback URLs from the panel above and send them to your Easybet affiliate contact. They plug these into their system. When a player converts, Easybet fires back to your tracker automatically.

3

Network postbacks are automatic

When an FTD postback is received, this tracker detects whether the click came from Mondiad or Propeller Ads and fires back to the correct network automatically.

4

Adding a new brand

Click + Add brand in the sidebar. Fill in name, slug (e.g. hollywoodbets), currency (ZAR, USD, EUR), pick a colour. Postback URLs are generated instantly.

Add Brand

Each brand gets its own colour-coded tracking, postback URLs and reports.
